## Notes & Observations

- The two highest-reach videos come from large channels covering Zig as news: **JetBrains** (228K subs) on Zig's 2026 No-AI policy / foundation funding / leaving GitHub, and **The PrimeTime** (1.13M subs) on Zig being "at a crossroads."
- **Zig vs Rust** is the dominant framing — many of the most-viewed videos are comparisons rather than pure tutorials.
- A cluster of dedicated small/medium creators publish regularly on Zig internals: **ComputerBread** (~5.5K subs), **Code With Cypert** (~8.5K), **Code Guild** (~8K), **TheTechyShop** (~2.1K), and the official-adjacent **Zig SHOWTIME** (~17K).
- Channel size and view count are weakly correlated: small channels (e.g. **codingjerk**, 12.1K subs) landed 100K+ view videos, showing strong topic interest can outweigh subscriber base.
